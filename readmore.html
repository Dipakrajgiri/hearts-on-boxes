About the Game

Remember the simple joy of playing games on paper and pen during your childhood? We sure do! Our online game, "Dots and Boxes," is a virtualized form of that nostalgic experience.

A Digital Twist to Childhood Fun

"Dots and Boxes" takes the classic pen-and-paper game and brings it to life in the digital realm. It's a strategic and entertaining game that challenges your wits and provides endless hours of fun.

How It Works

The game is created using a combination of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, making it accessible to anyone with a web browser. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, the virtual version captures the essence of the original while adding a modern touch.

Development Stack

Behind the scenes, the game is crafted using a combination of HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for dynamic functionality. This ensures a seamless and interactive gaming experience right in your browser.
