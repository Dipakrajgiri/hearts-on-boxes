How to Play - Dots and Boxes Game Help

Welcome to the Dots and Boxes online game!

This classic childhood favorite is now available for you to enjoy online. Click the start button to begin your adventure. Below, we've provided a guide on how to play and make the most of your gaming experience.

Help Desk:

If you're ready to dive into the game, click the start button. You'll be greeted with a game board where all the boxes are initially blue. Follow the steps below to get started.

Getting Started:

  1. Click the start button to play the game.
  2. The game board will appear with all boxes colored blue.

Taking Turns:

The game is played between two players, taking turns to make a move. Both players take turns selecting sides alternately. Click on an edge of a box to claim it. The box will change from blue to green, indicating it is now part of your territory. If the selected side completes a box, the respective player earns a point and a bonus chance.

Scoring:

Completing a square earns the player a point. The player who completes a square gets an additional turn. The hearts inside the completed squares change color: red for one player and yellow for the other.

Winning:

The game ends when all possible squares are formed. The player with the most points is declared the winner.

Tips:

  • Strategize to claim more squares and deny your opponent opportunities.
  • Pay attention to the color of the hearts to keep track of your progress.

Have Fun!

Now that you know the basics, dive into the world of Dots and Boxes. Enjoy the friendly competition and have fun completing those squares!

If you have any further questions or encounter issues, don't hesitate to contact us.
